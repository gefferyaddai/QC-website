Our Mission

Built by students,
for the markets.

UC Quants is a multidisciplinary student society at the University of Calgary uniting programmers, financial analysts, and researchers under one roof.

We bridge theory and practice — from Black-Scholes to live backtesting, from probability puzzles to quant job offers.

See what we do
Programmers

Build the technical infrastructure — coding environments, APIs, trading simulation platforms, and quantitative model implementations in Python.

Financial Analysts

Acquire, clean, and organize financial data. Identify relevant data sources and support feature engineering and analytical workflows.

Researchers

Develop quantitative investment ideas, explore financial theory, coordinate backtesting, and present findings to the organization.
