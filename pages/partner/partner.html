Work with the next

Whether you're a quant, trader, researcher, or industry professional — UC Quants is looking to connect with people who want to share their experience, challenge our thinking, and help shape what we build next.

Get Involved
How You Can Get Involved

Ways to connect
with us.

We're not looking for a transaction — we're looking for people genuinely interested in developing the next wave of quantitative thinkers. Here's how that can look.

Guest Speaker

Come talk to our members about your career path, a topic you're passionate about, or what the day-to-day of working in quant finance actually looks like.

Mentor a Member

Offer one-on-one or group mentorship to students navigating internship searches, technical interviews, or early career decisions in finance and tech.

Collaborate on Projects

Have a research question or dataset you'd like explored? Partner with our members on a real project and see what a motivated student team can do.

Run a Workshop

Lead a hands-on session — whether it's a mock interview, a Python workshop, a case study, or a deep dive into a concept you know well.

Who We're Looking For

You don't need a
big title.

We're open to connecting with anyone who has real experience and a genuine interest in helping students grow. That includes:

  • Quantitative analysts, researchers, and traders
  • Software engineers working in fintech or data
  • Portfolio managers and risk professionals
  • Alumni working in finance, tech, or consulting
  • Anyone with a story worth sharing
What to expect
Flexible commitment One session or ongoing — entirely up to you.
Engaged audience Members who are motivated, curious, and prepared.
In-person or virtual We work around your schedule and location.
No strings attached This isn't a recruitment pitch. Just a genuine connection.
